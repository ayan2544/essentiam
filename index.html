Strategic planning is the beginning

Sustainable growth comes from your people

Essentiam is the Latin word that gave us “essential.”

A word that perfectly describes your people.

Their collective, committed energy will drive your organization’s growth.

We provide strategic guidance and facilitation, uniting the diverse perspectives of your leadership team into common ground, a shared vision, and a collaborative path forward.

The process is inclusive, straightforward, and inspirational. We’ve built a practice based on our global experience that enables you to see beyond your current horizons and translate big picture thinking into achievable outcomes.

who we work with

If you have a team that leads your organization, we can help.

ASSOCIATIONS

We get it. We’ve done it.

We’ve worked as staff, volunteers, board members, and consultants. The unique dynamics of membership organizations mean success requires getting lots of people aligned and keeping them moving in the same direction. We can get you there.

BUSINESSES

Small, medium, large, we’ve been there.

With experience across more than 45 industries, we get where you are coming from. Manufacturing, services, retail, wholesale, law, insurance, science, education – check. We don’t specialize in a single industry. We leverage ideas and successful practices from across industries to accelerate growth.

NON-PROFITS

We understand. We’ve helped DOZENS!

Outcomes are everything for nonprofits. Being efficient with time, energy, and resources means you can make a bigger impact. We bring your board and staff behind a clearly articulated direction that donors, partners, and the public can get behind. It makes all the difference.

The most powerful ideas come from bringing people together

Strategic planning is the most common springboard for exploring big ideas and engaging in critical conversations – courageously, and as a team.

The magic happens when we create deliberate, structured interactions that engage your teams – inside and out – to generate new ideas from diverse perspectives. Together we address current challenges and build strategies that accelerate growth.
